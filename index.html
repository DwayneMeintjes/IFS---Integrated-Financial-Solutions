About Us

Mission

Provide the best quality professional, personal and business service.

Our Company

Integrated Financial Services was founded in 2016.


We are an assertive firm providing our clients a full range of financial and accounting services.


Our offices are located in Rustenburg. We do however render a wide variety of services to clients located throughout Gauteng and North West Province.


Because our firm is smaller than the norm, we maintain a close and open relationship with each of our clients. Our commitment is to provide the highest quality service, while retaining a smalltown atmosphere.


IFS is a member of the Southern African Insitute for Business Accountants (SAIBA), the fastest growing professional institute in South Africa, as well as a member of the South African Insitute of Tax Professionals (SAIT).

Director

Wayne Meintjes

Wayne grew up in Lichtenburg. He studied at the University of Pretoria, where he completed his B.Com Accounting Degree in 1993 and his B.Com Honours degree in Accounting Science in 1996 through UNISA. He then completed his trainee contract in 1997 with KPMG.


He joined the corporate mining sector for the period 1997 till 2004. He then entered the Construction Industry. He completed the Damelin course in Advanced Project Management in 2010 and has been involved in projects ever since. He became the director of IFS in 2016, applying his accounting and management skills obtained.


Wayne is a member of the Southern African Insitute of Business Accountants (SAIBA) and the South African Institute of Tax Professionals (SAIT).
